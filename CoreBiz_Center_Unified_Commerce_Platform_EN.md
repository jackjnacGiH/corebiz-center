# CoreBiz Center

## Unified Commerce Platform Specification

### (All-in-One E-Commerce, Inventory, CRM, Omni-Channel & Sales System)

CoreBiz Center is a centralized business platform designed to unify all
commerce operations into one easy-to-use system for business owners,
administrators, sales teams, inventory staff, marketing teams,
accounting, shipping departments, and sales agents.
